What payment methods are supported?

Most major US and international credit cards are accepted. All payments are handled by Stripe.

Can I switch my plans after subscribing?

You can switch your plan at any time. Your API token does not need to be changed when switching to another plan.

Do you have any options for open-source projects?

I have an open-source plan available which mirrors the free plan but with a higher call limit. If you are the creator or maintainer of a project currently using AVWX, email me with a link to your project repo.

What if I need a higher call limit?

If you need more than what the Enterprise plan provides, email me so we can make a custom plan that fits your specific use-case.
